“Lightning Talks” are short presentation meant to introduce a topic to audiences of all backgrounds.

These talks will be entirely student-run, which means that anyone is welcome to sign up to give a talk on any topic they’d like (provided that it’s related to mathematics!), and anyone is welcome to attend!

If you’d like to give a talk, sign up at https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1WT2ij8aJgI170WBVfwBI0R87IfXmCovCEORtgEXBC7U/edit#gid=0

As usual, there will also be raffle for a free Math book!
